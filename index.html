Welcome to the Weird & Wonderful!

You've stumbled upon Gooners, Minneapolis's best-kept secret (or perhaps its most openly bizarre culinary adventure). We pride ourselves on serving up dishes that will make you question everything you thought you knew about food. Come on in, grab a local brew, and prepare for an experience your tastebuds won't soon forget... for better or worse.

Who Are These Gooners Anyway?

Legend has it that Gooners was founded on a dare during a particularly harsh Minneapolis winter. Two friends, fueled by lukewarm coffee and an excess of tater tots, decided the city needed a place that wasn't afraid to be... different. We embrace the odd, the unconventional, and the downright strange. Our chefs (some call them culinary mad scientists) are escapees from the mundane, dedicated to crafting flavors that dance on the precipice of genius and "what were they thinking?!"

Nestled in the heart of Minneapolis, we're a haven for the adventurous eater, the curious connoisseur, and anyone who believes that a good meal should come with a great story. We source locally when we can, especially our pickles (you'll see why). We're a bit rough around the edges, just like a true Minnesotan after a blizzard, but we promise good times, strange bites, and maybe a new favorite dish you never knew you needed.
